8-4-25 - Web Design Museum

Web Design Museum (No "the") is a site and organisation that showcases interesting webpages through the history of the internet. It's pretty neat! Lots of interesting categories that show design trends through the years. They also catalogue apps and software and such. They also provide internet archive links to a few of the sites which is great, even if they don't necessarily work properly. The site does have a lot of ads on it, but I suppose that is a part of web design history too. Here's the link if you care.
