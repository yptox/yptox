6-4-25 - Hugh F, The Great Divides, & Strawberry Prince

I had the pleasure of seeing a few great musical acts at The Old Bar. I don't know how old it actually is, but it's a pretty good name for a bar regardless. Hugh played some splendid folk songs that made me feel very warm and smiley. The Great Divides were fantastic - any band with a saxophone and a violin is a band i like very much. I particularly enjoyed their song European Domination. I too often do the dishes late. Strawberry Prince and his band played some songs that were at points both delightfully reserved and emotional, and ear-splittingly heavy. I loved it! I had a really great time. I need to go to live music acts every day forever.
