15-4-25 - My Sweet Lord

This is another music one because I suppose I have been lovin it recently. Holy crap this song makes me feel amazing. Immediately you start with some pleasant guitar strumming, I've heard this a million times, I know what's coming up. Then it hits you. that beautiful strange electric guitar. Then Georgie boy gets on the mic. Is he singing about God? Is he singing about love? Is he in love with God? Do I need to find religious meaning in my life? This song makes me want to. Holy crap. I really want to see you lord. Really want to see you lord, but it takes so long. My lord. My sweet lord. Halleluljah.
